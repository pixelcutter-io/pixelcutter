Doublem is the CV website of our Maurizio, designed to showcase his passion for design and development, two aspects that coexist in harmony within him and give life to unique and recognizable projects.

![enter image description here](/images/doublem_2.png)

Rather than dwelling on the “what” or the “how,” the structure of the site guides users toward understanding the “why” behind each project. This approach allows visitors to enter the creator’s mindset, highlighting motivations, choices, and values that make each work personal and distinctive.

![enter image description here](/images/doublem_3.png)

Doublem is not just a showcase of Maurizio’s skills, but a living, evolving tool. Each update helps narrate his professional growth and continuous commitment to improvement, offering users a complete and up-to-date view of his creative and technical journey.
